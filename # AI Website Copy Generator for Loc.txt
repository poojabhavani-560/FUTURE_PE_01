## 3. Prompt Framework (Reusable)

### Master Prompt:

"Generate high-converting website copy for a [business type] located in [location]. Include homepage, services, and CTA sections. Use a [tone type] tone. Focus on benefits, trust, and local relevance. Avoid generic content."

### Inputs:

* Business Type
* Location
* Target Audience
* Services Offered
* Tone (friendly/professional)
